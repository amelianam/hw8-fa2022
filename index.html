About me!

Hi my name is Amelia Nam and I am a junior in the BSI program at the University of Michigan. I am on the UX Design pathway (user experience). My degree provides a unique skill set and is user-centered design, prioritizing the needs and experiences of the system's users. I am eager to create new products and experiences that are transformative for users!

My interest in Formula 1 started in 2020 when the pandemic first started out. When my family and I were quaranitining, we were looking for some shows to watch on Netflix. That is where we stumbled across this show called, Formula 1: Drive to Survive. My younger brother, dad, and I fell in love with the show!

I had always been interested in sports, but something about Formula 1 racing compelled me in a different way. I am excited to share more about my favorites teams and drivers! Stay tuned!
