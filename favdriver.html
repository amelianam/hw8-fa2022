Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton: On and Off the Track

Lewis Carl Hamilton is a British race-car driver. He is one of the most successful, if not the most successful F1 driver. In 2008 he became the first black driver to win the F1 world drivers' championship, he has gone on to win the Driver's constructor 6 more times! He has a joint record of 7 world driver's championship titles.

Lewis Hamilton is currently a co-owner of the Denver Broncos (professional football team)!

Lewis Hamilton empowers people and continues to educate and show us what the F1 community and even our society as a whole is lacking. The F1 community has a long history of having a lack of diversity and representation. Lewis Hamilton continues to advocate for the black and impoverished community.
